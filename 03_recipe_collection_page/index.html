High Protein Pancakes

These high-protein pancakes are fluffy, delicious, and packed with fuel to keep you energized. Perfect for a healthy breakfast or post-workout meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats (or oat flour)
  • 1 scoop protein powder (vanilla or plain works best)
  • 1 large egg (or 2 egg whites)
  • ½ cup milk (any type – dairy or plant-based)
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch of cinnamon (optional)
  • Sweetener of choice (like honey, stevia, or a ripe banana)

Instructions

  1. Mix oats, protein powder, egg, and milk in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan and lightly grease it.
  3. Pour batter and cook on medium heat until bubbles form, then flip.
  4. Cook the other side until golden.
  5. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.

Salmon Plate

A simple, healthy, and flavorful dish—juicy salmon paired with crisp, colorful veggies like broccoli and carrots for a balanced meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • head broccoli (cut into florets)
  • 1–2 carrots (sliced)
  • 1 zucchini (sliced, optional)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon (sliced or juiced)
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Optional: herbs like dill, parsley, or thyme

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Place salmon fillets on a baking tray lined with paper.
  3. Toss broccoli, carrots, and zucchini with olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper, then spread around salmon.
  4. Squeeze lemon juice over salmon and place lemon slices on top.
  5. Bake for 15–20 minutes, until salmon is cooked through and veggies are tender.
  6. Serve hot, topped with fresh herbs if desired.

Citrus Sunshine Mocktail

A refreshing, vibrant mocktail that’s sweet, tangy, and perfect for any occasion—sunshine in a glass!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup orange juice (fresh or bottled)
  • ½ cup pineapple juice
  • ½ cup sparkling water (or soda water)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Ice cubes
  • Orange slices and mint leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Pour in orange juice, pineapple juice, and lemon juice.
  3. Top up with sparkling water and stir gently.
  4. Garnish with an orange slice and fresh mint.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy!
