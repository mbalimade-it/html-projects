Who is she?

Hi there, my name is Mbali and I love to code. I am originally from South Africa and now reside in Belgium.

A mysterious language that led to curiosity

My interest in tech began when I was working for a tech company based in Belgium. Although I was not in a tech role, I became interested in learning the "cool stuff" they were doing—the "mysterious" language they were speaking amongst each other. Well, lo and behold, curiosity took over, and now I am here, learning and growing.

If I cannot see it, I cannot build it

What excites me the most about this journey is that I am a very visual/practical learner. This allows me to actually visualize and see what I'm trying to achieve. If I cannot see it, I cannot build it. It excites me to be able to break down a problem and solve it with code.

Why I love to code in a nutshell:

  • See what I am building in real time.
  • Room for growth, as I love to learn.
  • Code is code—there’s no misinterpretation.
  • Structure.
