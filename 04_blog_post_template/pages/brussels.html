Waffle Tasting in Brussels

Brussels is the waffle capital of Belgium, and I’d love for you to join me for a sweet adventure tasting some of its best waffles. Whether you’re a foodie or just curious to try something new, this event is all about enjoying delicious flavors, stories, and laughs together.

"Brussels is often called the heart of Europe, famous for its stunning Grand Place and world-class beer culture." - European Travel Guide

What to Expect:

  • A guided tasting of both Brussels and Liège waffles (and maybe a few surprises).
  • Tips on how to spot the best waffle stands in the city.
  • A chance to taste different toppings—from classic powdered sugar to fresh fruit and chocolate.
  • A relaxed walk through charming Brussels streets while enjoying waffles along the way.

Why Brussels?

Brussels is the perfect place to taste the difference between its light, crispy waffles and the caramelized sweetness of Liège waffles. Along the way, you’ll also get to explore the heart of the city with its lively squares and cozy corners.

Practical Info

Location: Grand Place, Brussels

Date & Time: 10 January 2030, 2:00pm

Cost: €35 pp
