Beer Tasting in Leuven

Leuven is the beer capital of Belgium, and I’d love for you to join me for a cozy evening of tasting some of its best brews. Whether you’re a long-time beer lover or just curious to try something new, this event is all about sharing good drinks, stories, and laughs together.

"Leuven is the beer capital of Belgium, home to the world-famous Stella Artois brewery." – Visit Leuven Guide

The brewery is described as historic and iconic by beer enthusiasts.

What to Expect:

  • A guided tasting of classic Belgian beers (and maybe a few surprises).
  • A short introduction to the history of Leuven and its world-famous breweries.
  • Notes on how to taste like a pro—looking at aroma, flavor, and style.
  • A relaxed setting where you can meet new people and enjoy the atmosphere.

Why Leuven?

Leuven is home to some of the biggest and most historic breweries in the world, as well as smaller craft gems that you don’t want to miss. It’s the perfect place to raise a glass and discover what makes Belgian beer so special.

Practical Info

Location: Stella Artois, Leuven

Date & Time: 22 December 2029, 11:00am

Cost: €50 pp

Don't forget to bring your curiosity and your taste buds—it’s going to be a great evening!
